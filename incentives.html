Available Incentive & Grant Programs



  • Economic hardship extension test
  • CalCAPloan access program
  • Prop 1B: The Goods Movement Emission Reduction Funding Program


  • Economic hardship extension test - The California Air and Resources Board offers financial assistance to fleets struggling to comply with increasingly stressful regulations. The economic hardship test asks eight simple questions to ascertain whether or not a fleet is eligible for an economic hardship extension, which allows a longer time frame to comply to diesel fuel regulations. This option is only for fleets that have failed to comply with 2014 standards, and must be accepted by the arb even once meeting the minimum requirements.

  • Economic hardship extension test - The CalCAP Capital Access Program offers programs such as loan insurance and assistance to small firms across California. CalCAP offers up to 100% coverage on loan defaults by encouraging banks and other large financial and state institutions to donate to or invest in smaller California businesses who may not have easy access to financing. CalCap has proven effective towards helping many California small businesses.

  • Prop 1B: The Goods Movement Emission Reduction Funding Program - Prop 1B (although currently closed) is a program, administered by the California ARB, that supplements funds towards the purchase newer trucks through both loans for purchase as well as lease-to-own loan programs.

What does any of this mean?



  • What are the 2010 engine-equivalent standards?
  • Here is another informative question


  • The 2010 engine equivalent standards are limits upon the release of certain measured pollutants from a given motor. These measured pollutants include the levels of particulate matter, nitrogen oxide, hydrocarbons, and carbon monoxide. An important set goal of the cap and trade system includes ensuring that the fleets upgrade their engines to the emissions standards based upon model engines manufactured in 2010.
